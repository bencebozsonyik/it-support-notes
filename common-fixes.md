# Common Fixes

## Printer not printing
**Symptoms**
- Printer shows offline
- Print jobs stuck in queue

**Troubleshooting**
1. Restart Print Spooler service
2. Clear print queue
3. Check cables and network connection

**Resolution**
Printer returned online and printed successfully.

---

## No sound
**Symptoms**
- No sound
- Device not detected

**Troubleshooting**
1. Check speaker/headphone connections
2. Check Windows sound settings
3. Update sound card driver

**Resolution**
Updating the driver fixed the issue.
